MENU

STARTERS

Paneer Tikka

Succulent cubes of paneer marinated in yogurt and aromatic spices, grilled to perfection and served with mint chutney.

Papdi Chaat

Crisp papdis layered with chickpeas, potatoes, yogurt, and a medley of chutneys, garnished with sev and pomegranate seeds for a delightful crunch.

Main course

Butter Chicken

Tender pieces of chicken simmered in a rich and creamy tomato-based gravy, infused with butter and aromatic spices, served with fragrant basmati rice.

Palak Paneer

Fresh spinach and paneer cooked in a creamy blend of spices, creating a luscious and nutritious dish that's a favorite among vegetarians.

Dal Makhani

Slow-cooked black lentils simmered with butter, cream, and aromatic spices, resulting in a rich and velvety texture that pairs perfectly with steamed rice.

Breads

Garlic Naan

Soft and fluffy bread infused with garlic and brushed with butter, perfect for sopping up flavorful curries.

Peshawari Naan

Sweet and nutty naan bread filled with a delicious mixture of dried fruits, nuts, and aromatic spices, offering a delightful twist to traditional Indian bread.

Desserts

Gulab Jamun

Soft and spongy milk dumplings soaked in a sweet syrup infused with cardamom and rose water, offering a decadent finale to your meal.

Kulfi

Traditional Indian ice cream made with thickened milk, flavored with saffron, cardamom, and nuts, served on a stick for a refreshing and indulgent treat.

Rasgulla

Delicate cottage cheese dumplings soaked in a light and fragrant sugar syrup, offering a light and refreshing end to your culinary journey.

Beverages

Mango Lassi

A creamy and refreshing yogurt-based drink blended with ripe mangoes and a hint of cardamom, offering a perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess.

Masala Chai

Aromatic Indian tea brewed with a blend of spices such as cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves, simmered with milk and sugar for a comforting and flavorful experience.
